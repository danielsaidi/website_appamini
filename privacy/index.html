Privacy Policy

This is the privacy policy for Appamini's iOS apps. This includes Appamini ABC and Appamini 123.

Appamini ABC and Appamini 123 are hereby referred to as "the apps".

Whenever this policy mentions "personal data", it refers to data that directly or indirectly may be used to identify you as an individual. Whenever this policy mentions "anonymous data", it refer to data that can not be used to identify you as an individual.

In short:

  • The apps do not collect any personal data.
  • The apps do not actively collect any anonymous data.
  • The apps do not track how users use the apps.

The apps can share diagnostics, crash reports etc. to Apple, but that is true for all apps that you download from the App Store. This data collection is handled by Apple, not Appamini, and requires your explicit approval.

If any of this information changes with a new release, the release notes will mention that this privacy policy has been updated.
