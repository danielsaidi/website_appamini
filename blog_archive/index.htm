Italian audio recorded

 

Tonight, we have recorded audio for the next version of Appamini ABC, together with the mega-talented Alice. She will voice the Italian mode, which means that the next version will feature three languages – English, Swedish and Italian.

We will also re-record the Swedish audio, to fix the confusing recording where some letters sound similar. We will also re-record the English audio, to fill out some gaps for images that we are going to add. These updates will, however, be added somewhat later than the Italian mode.

Other than that, the app will feature several improvements and new things to play around with. We hope to release it soon.

Appamini ABC get’s reviewed by Pappasappar

Appamini ABC just got a three star review, a recommendation badge and valuable feedback from pappasappar.se, a Swedish app review site that focuses on children apps.

The reviewer describes Appamini ABC like this: “Appamini ABC is a game that focuses on the Swedish and the English alphabet. With the help of imaginative illustrations and three different play stations, children learn letters, how they look and how they sound.”

The review is overall good, and the reviewer also commented on some areas of improvement, which we appreciate a lot. It is very valuable to have for the upcoming versions, where we will work on improving the game moments further.

Check out the full review at pappasappar.se - http://www.pappasappar.se/appamini-abc/

Appamini ABC improvements

We are currently working intensively with the next version of Appamini ABC, and would like to share our plans with you.

The biggest feature in the new version is that we will add the Italian language. That means that you will be able to play the games and read the book in Italian as well.

Molto buone!

In addition to this, we will add new images to the book, tweak the tutorials to make the games easier to understand, add more hidden things and make some smaller improvements that will improve the overall user experience.

The new version will be released during spring.
